Description

Crafter guitars are dearly loved by musicians all over the world. In Russia, the brand's instruments have long held a leading position in sales, as customers firmly believe that Crafter represents a perfect combination of superior sound characteristics, high-quality wood, and flawless design. This is an entry-level series of electric guitars featuring a Stratocaster body made of mahogany. All guitars are equipped with a Wilkinson two-point tremolo bridge and a mint green pickguard. The frets are made of stainless steel. Every guitar in this series features Alnico 5 pickups and a mini-toggle switch for humbucker-to-single-coil splitting. Electronics configuration: HSS, 1 Volume, 1 Tone, and a 5-way selector switch.
